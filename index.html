Battle Farm Galactica is the album that pioneered the sound and genre of Spirit-Punk, meticulously crafted to leave the listener in a state of existential WTF-ism.

It's like meditation, crystals, synthesizers, and drum machines have crashed head first into raw and edgy guitar riffs, causing a terrible accident that you can't look away from.

The lyrics might be a diss-track about reality.
